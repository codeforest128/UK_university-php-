About us 

Oxbridge Careers Hub is a service created by students and graduates of Oxbridge and Durham, aiming to increase the efficiency of the job searching process for students, while helping companies find and recruit Britain's top talent. We know from our experience as students looking for work that this can be a stressful and frustrating task. To solve this we allow companies to search for, and contact students.

We work with exciting partners who know how important top talent is to the success of a business. Our site gives them unrivalled direct contact with exclusively Oxbridge, Durham and Imperial students looking for work. For students, it gives them information and potential opportunities to apply to these companies directly.

How it works

Students can join the database in a matter of minutes. They can choose to give more detailed information if they wish to give employers a better insight into them as candidates. Employers have a platform with which they can easily view multiple candidates' information and contact them directly. From this point on, contact is had directly with the candidate and we step out of the process.
