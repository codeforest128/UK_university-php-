FAQ

Is Oxbridge Careers Hub officially affiliated with the University of Oxford, University of Oxford Careers service or Internship Office?

No, we are not affiliated with any of these groups.

Are there opportunities available for this Summer and the start of next year?

Yes, many employers use our site looking for students available then.

I'm an Oxbridge/Durham Graduate , can I still sign up to be contacted employers?

Yes, simply sign up using your graduate email address, more information about that can be found here.

Who can view my information?

We only allow companies registered on our site to view your CV and data.

How do I delete my profile?

A button to delete your account is included in the bottom left of your profile page.

How will companies contact me?

Companies can call or email you directly, from this point on we step out of the process.

Is it only for Oxbridge Students?

No, Durham students can sign up as well

Do I still have to apply normally to companies that contact me?

It varies from company to company. Some will ask you to interview and others may ask you to apply through the normal process.
